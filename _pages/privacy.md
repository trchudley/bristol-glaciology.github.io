## Analytics

We use [Plausible Analytics](https://plausible.io/) to understand how this site is used. Plausible is a privacy-friendly, open-source analytics tool that:

- **collects no personal data** and sets no cookies
- does not track you across sites or devices
- is fully compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and PECR — no cookie banner required

Our Plausible instance is self-hosted, so no data is shared with third-party analytics companies. The only information recorded is aggregate, anonymous page-view counts.
